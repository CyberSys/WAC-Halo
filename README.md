WAC-Halo
===============

A collection of aircraft based around Halo vehicles made for WAC by Dr. Matt and commissioned by 'The Mendicant'.

Installation
===============
1. Put this entire folder into Steam/Steamapps/USERNAME/garrysmod/garrysmod/addons/
2. Done, enjoy your flight.

Aircraft
===============
In this pack you will find:

• UH-144 Falcon
• AV-14 Hornet
• D77H-TCI Pelican
